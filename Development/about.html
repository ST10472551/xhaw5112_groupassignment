Our Story

Born from a Vision of Equal Opportunity

Founded in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering the Nation emerged from a vision to bridge the skills gap in domestic work and gardening sectors.

We recognized that many talented individuals lacked access to professional training opportunities that could enhance their employability and earning potential.

Our programs are designed by industry experts and delivered by experienced instructors who understand the real-world needs of these professions.
